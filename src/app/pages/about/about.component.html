Pappala Dabba

Pappala Dabba is more than just an e-commerce platform; it's a heartfelt endeavor to bring the rich culinary heritage of Andhra Pradesh to your doorstep. Born from a deep love for traditional sweets and snacks, our journey began with a simple vision: to share authentic, homemade flavors that evoke nostalgia and create new memories.

Every recipe is a testament to generations of expertise, carefully passed down and perfected. We meticulously select the finest ingredients, ensuring purity and unparalleled taste in every single product. From the bustling kitchens of our ancestors to your modern home, we bridge the gap with quality, tradition, and a touch of magic.

The Name

In 1980-90s, our grannies used to keep all those special sweets in a Dabba; a telugu word (English; container) and they call it as a Pappala Dabba. We as children used to request, cry, fight to get the sweets from those dabbas. We sometimes end up doing all mischievous activities to get those sweets from dabbas. So our name Pappala Dabba evolves from those memories.

When people (especially telugu people) listen’s to our platform name (Pappala Dabba),we are sure they will remember their childhood days at least once and they will explain the same with kids as well.
